About the Project

In this project, we take few hundred records which includes information about various heart patients over time. The data includes records of patients over 30 years of age along with other information, like systolic and diastolic pressure, cholesterol level, blood glucose level, addiction over smokes and alcohols and fitness. On the basis of that, we use machine learning techniques to determine whether that particular person is having a risk of heart related issues or not. We then create a web app which takes input from the user and on the basis of that, the predicted outcome (result) is generated and displayed to the user.
